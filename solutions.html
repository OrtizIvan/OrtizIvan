Botany & Crispr-Cas

Genediting

We love and need trees like little else in the world. They reassure us with theirs Leaves, their green relaxes us, their flowers often spread a pleasant scent, fruit and Nut trees provide us with food, we use their wood as a renewable raw material, they offer countless things a habitat for other species and without photosynthesis there would be no higher life on our planet. But trees are also some of the most threatened creatures on our planet. Deforestation , Climate change , They suffer from a lack of water and parasites. But what solutions have we got so far and in the Future available to face these problems? In silviculture this question comes with a long one temporal component and a significant rethink. Spruce trees were fast because of their speed Until 30 years ago it was the most popular tree species in the forest. However, spruce trees in the lowlands and the low mountain ranges have always been alien to the location and accordingly little resistant. Beeches have long been considered suitable for climate change, but it could be too Difficulties arise when it increases longer dry episodes . The decision which tree species to plant is therefore not an easy one. to The developments in regional climatic conditions over the next 80-100 years are still unclear. It is therefore considered Mediterranean trees (French Maple ) or of American origin (Douglas fir ) to plant that can cope with drier and hotter conditions and not so remote from the location are that the local fauna could not adapt.
Another factor that will determine the choice of future species is a direct consequence of it Climate change and globalization: the infestation with Parasites brings some species such as ash trees, Elms or the American chestnut on the verge of extinction. In the Food production threatens Panama Illness or Citrus Crab wipe out some of the most popular fruits like lemons, oranges and bananas. Genetic engineering and especially gene editing (i.e. changing the genome of plant species without foreign genes) could provide solutions in these cases that can no longer be found through natural breeding possible are. While in the range of Crispr-Cas in relation to the application in humans and animals the Approval is rightly still controversial due to the increased risk of cancer, are with plants Mutations are much less likely. However, one should take into account that gene editing is still a very is a young field of research and, accordingly, tests for long-term effects have rarely been carried out. It is correspondingly difficult to weigh up risks 1,2. It would therefore be advisable to carry out field tests first with Tree species and plants to carry out, which are doomed to extinction without genetic editing and are of great importance for the Have biodiversity or food security. However, this approach is often contrary to the interests of companies aiming for profit from already high performing crops like wheat. Here are just those of these Companies also want adjustments due to drought or salt resistance through gene editing their complexity cannot be implemented, which even Entwicklungsbiologen bestätigt wird. Vor dem Hintergrund von Waldsterben und Klimawandel ist auch die natürliche Selektion wieder in den Fokus von Wissenschaftlern geraten. Denn bei jeder Art gibt es auch unterschiedliche Phänotypen, die sich u.a. auch durch divergierende Gene characteristics to make noticable. So it should be considered whether it is better to work in forests, but also near street trees, not even with Planting individuals of trees with different phenotypes planted together after 30-40 years then to be able to determine the more competitive in the respective situation.

Connectivity

Companies

In a globalized world, networking is the key currency. Globalized production and Supply chains, globalized transport, data exchange, servers that host our knowledge. But also Friendships across continents. All of this makes our modern life possible. And of course it has there also side effects. Analyzing and assessing these has been an increasingly important trend in business since 2004. With the help of ESG, investments in companies or stock portfolios are classified according to social and environmentally relevant criteria such as gender equality or sustainability risks. This Step is important and right to have a tool with that not just individual companies, but even entire production chains with suppliers across different ones Can be assessed for their sustainable production methods across countries. This can not only Production methods compared, but also investments depending on environmental and social aspects be made. However, the question arises whether companies often not only certify practices that they are based on would have implemented economic and image reasons anyway? Here I am divided in my assessment. On the one hand, organizations like the Global Reporting Initiative GRI included Establish sustainability standards and collect and share knowledge. On the other hand, evaluations with regard to sustainable business are still negative Rating versehen (e.g. through the PRI portrayed for a Chilean mining company, which due to legal regulations Must use seawater desalination plants to save fresh water). There is no question about that this could of course reduce the return on investment for companies. At the same time, however, the question is raised how we do it in a capitalist economic system prevent sustainable measures from having a negative impact on the return assessment? Here one should consider whether it should not apply to all (!) Economic goods that are produced in the world can a global and transparent production chain be represented at least digitally? The approach established by the ESG shows that this would be possible in principle. This does not mean that, in principle, all decisions that a company makes are sustainable have to. Instead, there would often be more benefits if companies like Siemens on the one hand, their technology also allows them to be used for the extraction of raw materials, but on the other hand, for this purpose committed to developing technologies and implementing measures that result from these actions emerging Follow-up costs reduced again. Trading with Emissions was a first step for this, but it was far too short-sighted and would have been implemented much more stringently Need to become. However, there is no point in setting rigid quotas that the state of the art does not gives away. It is better and more sensible for companies not only in the construction sector, but in all of them To oblige environmentally harmful areas to implement compensatory measures and their implementation to accompany permanently.
